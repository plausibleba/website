Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version

PlausibleBA does not collect, store, or process any personal data. The skills run entirely within your Claude Cowork session. We have no servers, no databases, no analytics, and no tracking.

What PlausibleBA is

PlausibleBA is an open-source skills library for Claude Cowork, published at github.com/plausibleba/ba-skills. When you install and run PlausibleBA skills, they operate as part of your Claude conversation — governed entirely by Anthropic's privacy policy, not ours.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information through the skills.
  • We do not collect the content of your conversations or the business descriptions you provide to the skills.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or cookies on this website beyond what is technically necessary to serve the pages.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

The PlausibleBA Canvas (plausibleba.com/canvas)

The Canvas page allows you to upload a ba-skills-bundle.json file and render it as an interactive operating model. This upload is processed entirely in your browser — the file is never sent to a server. We do not retain, log, or store any uploaded bundle data.

GitHub

The PlausibleBA skills library is hosted on GitHub at github.com/plausibleba/ba-skills. GitHub's own privacy policy applies to any interaction with that repository. We do not control GitHub's data practices.

Claude Cowork and Anthropic

When you use PlausibleBA skills within Claude Cowork, your conversation data is governed by Anthropic's privacy policy. PlausibleBA has no access to your Cowork conversations.

This website

plausibleba.com is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We use Plausible Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Plausible is privacy-preserving — it does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and is fully GDPR compliant. The data collected is aggregated only (page views, referrers, top pages) and cannot be used to identify individual visitors. You can review Plausible's data policy at plausible.io/privacy.

Standard server access logs (IP address, page requested, timestamp) may also be retained by GitHub Pages in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Children

PlausibleBA is a professional tool intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 16.

Changes to this policy

If we ever introduce any data collection (for example, an account system or analytics), we will update this policy and note the date of change prominently. The current version of this policy is always available at plausibleba.com/privacy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at hello@plausibleba.com.
