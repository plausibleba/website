About PlausibleBA

Built by practitioners, for practitioners. PlausibleBA encodes decades of business architecture and enterprise advisory experience into three slash commands.

The mission

Enlisting BAs to do business architecture

Business Analysts are already doing the work that business architecture formalises — they're eliciting requirements, mapping processes, talking to stakeholders, and documenting how the business operates. What they're often missing is the structural framework that makes those outputs durable, reusable, and enterprise-grade.

PlausibleBA's mission is to close that gap without asking BAs to become business architects. The skills encode BIZBOK methodology so the outputs are structurally correct by default — not because the BA knows BIZBOK, but because the skill does.

"Just enough business architecture" is the operating principle. Not comprehensive enterprise architecture. Not a full Guild certification. Just the three artefacts — capability map, concept model, value stream — that give every project a structured, validated foundation.
