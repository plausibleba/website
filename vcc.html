How it works

From model to actionable intelligence in four moves.

Generate your operating model on the free PlausibleBA Canvas, then open it in VCC for deep analysis.

1

Import your bundle

Click "Open in VCC" from the PlausibleBA Canvas, or upload any ba-skills-bundle.json. Your value stream, capability map, and concept model load instantly into the interactive stage view.

2

Assess friction

VCC runs AI-powered friction assessment across every stage of your value stream — identifying bottlenecks, handoff failures, data gaps, and governance blind spots. Each friction point is scored by severity and mapped to its root cause.

3

Map solutions

For every friction point, VCC suggests remediation options — from process changes to vendor solutions. Solutions are mapped to capabilities, so you can see exactly what each intervention affects across the operating model.

4

Plan the transformation

Model throughput projections, prioritise by impact, and build a sequenced transformation roadmap grounded in your actual operating model — not a disconnected spreadsheet.

Capabilities

Six analytical layers. One canvas.

Each layer builds on your PlausibleBA operating model. No re-entry. No re-drawing. Just analysis.

Friction Assessment

Find the bottlenecks

AI-powered analysis scores friction across every value stream stage. Identifies handoff failures, data gaps, skill shortages, and governance blind spots.

Binding Constraints

Find the one that matters most

Theory of Constraints analysis identifies the single binding constraint limiting throughput — so you invest in the right place, not everywhere at once.

Solution Mapping

Map remediation to capabilities

Every friction point gets matched to remediation options — process changes, technology solutions, or vendor products — mapped back to the capabilities they affect.

Throughput Modelling

Model the impact

Project throughput improvements for each intervention. Compare scenarios. Quantify the value of removing friction before you commit budget.

Transformation Planning

Sequence the work

Build a prioritised transformation roadmap grounded in your operating model. Dependencies, sequencing, and impact — all in one view.

Governance Controls

Keep it honest

Track observations, decisions, and rationale against each stage. Maintain an audit trail of analytical reasoning as the model evolves.
